Our Mission

A sanctuary of silence and prayer, grounded in the Gospel and compassion. Inspired by the Pool of Bethesda (John 5), our center is a place of healing, listening, and transformation.

History & Spirituality

Founded by a religious community, the center embodies living faith and Christian hospitality. It is inspired by the Word to offer rest and comfort.

Our Team

A team of spiritual guides welcomes you with kindness: priests, religious, and trained lay people offering listening and presence.
